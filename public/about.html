Our Family Story

We weave Indian traditions with modern western comfort, designing outfits that accommodate children as they grow and parents as they dress for the world.

Local Craft, Global Reach

Established in 2024, Little to Large started with a simple observation: children grow out of their outfits incredibly fast, and families struggle to find consistent, matching quality traditional outfits under one roof. We set out to create a unified family-centric clothing hub.

We collaborate directly with local weavers in Jodhpur, Varanasi, and Jaipur to create handcrafted pieces. By removing middlemen, we pay fair wages to artisans while providing customers worldwide with genuine heritage clothing.

Our custom fabric blends are 100% bio-washed organic cotton and linen, which prevents allergies on baby skin, while our adult silk collections feel light, royal, and breathable.

What Drives Us

Our guiding values shape every collection we design

👪 Family Inclusivity

From newborn sizing (0-3 Months) to adult plus sizes (XXL), we ensure every family member is represented with dignity and style.

🌱 Sustainable Cotton

Our clothes use biodegradable fibers, certified organic cotton, and vegetable dyes, preserving the environment for future generations.

🇮🇳 Weaver Empowerment

We reinvest 10% of all global proceeds directly into local crafting guilds across rural India, keeping heritage skills alive.
